About Me

Hello! My name is MPANo Aphrodis, and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2023 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes — turns out hacking together a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of working at a start-up, a huge corporation, and a student-led design studio. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences at Acme Corporation for a variety of clients.

I also recently launched a web app that covers everything you need to know about algorithms and data structures for coding interviews. I most frequently work with Python, Flask, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, C++, and Java to create robust and efficient applications.

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually cycling, hiking, or searching for the perfect cup of coffee.
